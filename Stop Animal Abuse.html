Stop Animal Abuse

Abuse or neglect of animals Simply put, “animal cruelty” refers to the abuse or neglect of animals. While cats, dogs, and other furry, feathery or scaly companions may come to mind when you hear the term animal cruelty, the animals we raise and slaughter for food are suffering from cruelty, too—and they are suffering by the billions, day in and day out.
